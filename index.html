Research Experience

SUNY Buffalo — November 2013 – Present

  • Should Smartphone Users Mock Apps? — In Proceedings of ACM HotPlanet 2014.
    Nick DiRienzo, Geoffrey Challen (SUNY Buffalo)
    Introduces the notion of smartphone data mocking to protect users’ private information, and discusses ethical issues and multiple ways of implementing such a feature.
  • Smartphone Users Want to Be Mocked — In Posters & Demoes of ACM HotMobile 2014.
    Nick DiRienzo, Gino Buzzelli, Geoffrey Challen (SUNY Buffalo)
    Describes the implementation of an Android platform-level system to allow users to better control their sensitive data by tricking installed apps into thinking the user is somewhere else, doing something else or being someone else. Awarded best poster.
